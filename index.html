About Us

Established in 1931, DND Group is an employee-owned, premier New Jersey-based construction firm with vast experience in healthcare, pharmaceutical, academic, commercial, industrial, financial, retail, institutional, manufacturing and other diverse construction projects.

Our experience includes, but is not limited to hospitals, pharmaceutical production facilities, office buildings, theaters, academic facilities, athletic complexes & fields, research labs, retail, warehouses and financial facilities.
